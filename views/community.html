Our Work For People and planet.

Our mission is dedicated to making a positive impact on both people and the planet.

How to be a volunteer?

Volunteering involves giving your time and abilities to help others, usually a community or non-profit organization. In the context of our organization, becoming a volunteer in the Scrape Community Leadership Program is an excellent way to gain practical experience. Reach out to the Scrape team expressing your interest and inquire about available roles that align with your skills. You will be guided by Scrape to familiarize yourself with their mission and procedures. As a volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to make a positive impact and contribute to our collective goals.

Take a look at these volunteering benefits
to help inspire you

  1. Meet New People:
    • Form lasting bonds with like-minded individuals working towards a common goal.
    • Connect with people who share similar interests and lifestyle.
  2. Learn About Other Ways of Life:
    • Gain exposure to new cultures and perspectives through diverse volunteering opportunities.
    • Enhance your worldview and social flexibility.
  3. Gain a Sense of Purpose and Happiness:
    • Experience fulfillment by donating your skills and time to help others.
    • Cultivate a sense of purpose, leading to increased happiness.
  4. Physical Health Benefits:
    • Reduce stress levels and boost mood by engaging in meaningful activities.
    • Enjoy various health advantages, including better sleep, a stronger immune system, and a healthier heart.
  5. Advance Your Skills and Career:
    • Adapt to working in teams and enhance problem-solving skills through diverse volunteer tasks.
    • Learn new skills, strengthen abilities, and boost your resume for potential career advancement.
